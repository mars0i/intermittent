Intermittent
Marshall Abrams

Cultural transmission with intermittent between-group communication and success bias. Inspired by BaliPlus in my bali repo. Each individual copies the most successful of those who talk to it on a tick. Neighbors (gray lines) always talk to each other. Others talk to others, randomly, as well. Transparent greenish lines represent an individual (wide) learning from another individual (narrow). Colors represent religious or other cultural variants. Little pointers represent an individual's current success, which is partly a function of its neighbors' religious values.
