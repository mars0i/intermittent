Intermittent
Marshall Abrams

Experiments with intermittent between-group communication, generalizing BaliPlus in my bali repo. Within-group communication is common, between-group communication is uncommon, with success bias: Each individual copies the most successful of those who talk to it on a tick. Neighbors (gray lines) always talk to each other. Others talk to others, randomly, as well. Green lines represent an individual (narrow) learning from another individual (wide). Colors represent religious or other cultural variants. Little pointers represent an individual's current success, which is partly a function of its neighbors' religious values.
